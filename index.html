A Brief History

The Harley-Davidson MT350s were designed and built from the early to late 1990's.

Based upon the earlier Armstrong MT500, HD improved the bikes with brake upgrades and electric start.

The MT350 was designed and built for the British armed forces and came with 2 front mounted panniers and a rear mounted gun box which gives the MT350 it's distinctive look.
